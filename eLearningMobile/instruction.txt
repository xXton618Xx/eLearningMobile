Game-Based E-Learning Application for Philippine Law
Context: A game-based learning application for the Philippine Law, dedicated for students of Political
Science and the students of Law themselves. This application aims to provide the students an easier
and more engaging way to explore, analyze, and manifest their desire in becoming a Lawyer, while
maintaining the utmost accuracy of the information present in the Philippine Law Procedures.

Major Components:
1. Sign-up / Login Page (for new and returning users)
    > Sign-up if new user
    > login if account logged out
    > otherwise when app opens, display splash when on load, route to dashboard/home page
2. Home Page (a.k.a. Dashboard)
    > Welcome Card
	> Welcome, 'user' if first login
	> Welcome back, 'user' if has account
    > General user statistics
	    > Daily hours per day
	    > Pie Chart: Completed / In Progress / Locked
    > Activity History
	    > Last Lesson in Progress
	    > Last Assessment in Progress
    > floating icon as sidebar (lessons page, interactive games page, settings page)
3. Lessons Page
    > Lesson Title
    > Combination of Texts and Images
	    > Start with Image related to lesson
	    > up to 4 images per lesson
    > Sources of Information (to be cited every after 'lesson')
        > web links (articles, websites, journals, etc.)
        > search the internet and scrape its relevant content based on the selected topics
    > Post-Lesson Assessment (Identification, True or False, Multiple Choice)
	    > Identification: accepts any-case strings of response, alphanumeric only (include '.' and '-')
	    > Multiple Choice: 4 choices, 1 correct answer
4. Interactive/Game Page (collective of all lessons)
    >  Matching Type (tap-based, no line draw)
	    > Minimal: 5 items
	    > Maximal: 10 items
	    - random arrangement
    > Fill in the blanks
	    > Identification-type (accepts any-case strings of response, alphanumeric only (include '.' and '-'))
        > swipe left or right [True or False]
	    > accumulate daily points, display daily:total
    > Point-based Assessments
	    > Minimal: 10 questions
	    > Manageable: 20 questions
	    > Maximal: 30 questions
	    - can exit and save progress
5. Miscellaneouses
    > Loading Page (simple rotating icon with  'Loading' text)
    > Icon design (user-defined, write code to place it)
    > Screen Splash (shown upon screen load)
6. Settings Page
    > System color theme (system light / dark)
    > user log out (routes back to log-in page)
    > Account deletion (Routes back to account creation page)
Topics to Include:
1. 1987 Philippine Constitution (all articles)
2. Presidents of 5th Republic of the Phil. and their history (1987 - Present)
3. Republic Acts (latest 10 signed, detailed)

Language Stack:
    Frontend: React Native
    Backend: Node.js / Express
    Database: MongoDB via MongoDB Atlas
